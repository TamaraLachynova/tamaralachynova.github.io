Who am I?

Tamara Lachynova, born on May 19th, 1994 in Kiew, Ukraine. Raised in Berlin, Germany where I currently live, study and work. Fluent in German, Russian and English. Graduated in 2012 from F.F. Runge Gymnasium in Oranienburg. Currently studying Graphic design at HTW Berlin. Fascinated by the human ability to process inspiration and to create, change and develop as a result. Always seeking for new inspiration, fascinating discoveries and experiences because there's still much place to grow.
